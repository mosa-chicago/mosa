Welcome to Midwest Open Source Alliance

Midwest Open Source Alliance promotes the education, support and development of Drupal and related open source software within the Midwest Drupal community (and beyond). We are an Illinois nonprofit and were granted federal 501(c)(3) tax exempt organization status in 2018 (EIN 83-1560285).

We are fiscal sponsor for:

  • Midwest Area Drupal Camp (MidCamp) - founding member
  • Twin Cities Drupal Camp - founding member
  • Midwest Drupal Summit - founding member
  • A11yTalks - member since 2024
  • Drupal Asheville - member since 2024
  • Decoupled Days - member since 2024
  • Stanford WebCamp - member since 2024
  • Pacific Northwest Drupal Summit - member since 2024
  • Drupal NJ - member since 2024
  • Drupal Diversity & Inclusion - member since 2024

In addition, we support meetups and initiatives that improve the event experience for both speakers and attendees, especially in areas that promote inclusivity and accessibility. We are currently supporting:

  • Drupal Recording Initiative
  • Live Captioning Initiative
  • Drupal Chicago Meetup group
